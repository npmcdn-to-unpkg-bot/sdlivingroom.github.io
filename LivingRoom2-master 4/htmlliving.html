A B O U T

With (2) locations to serve you, The Living Coffeehouse is one of the finest coffeehouse and bistro in the San Diego Area. With the backing of culinary masters from around the world, only The Living Room can offer such a wonderful array of gourmet meals, pastries, and drinks.

Since it's conception in 1991, the Living Room has grown from its original location in the San Diego State University area and expanded to La Jolla. Originally rooting from European foundations, the Living Room Coffeehouse has adjusted to SoCal, offering you a European coffeeshop with a Californian flair. Both locations offer gourmet food, great service, and quality experience. In addition, the La Jolla location offers a wide range of alcoholic beverages and hookah/hookah flavors.
